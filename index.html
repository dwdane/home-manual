v1.0.0

Reminders on your phone

This app is local-only, so it can't ping you while it's closed. The fix: export your schedule to your phone's calendar. Every task becomes a repeating all-day event with an alert, and the calendar does the nagging. Re-export after big schedule changes.

House profile

Change what the house has — new tasks are added for anything you turn on. Turning something off pauses its tasks rather than deleting history.

Backup

Everything lives on this device only. Nothing is uploaded. Export a backup before anything risky, and to copy the setup to another phone.

History

Updates

The app updates itself when a new version is deployed. Fully close and reopen it and you'll get an "update ready" banner.

Clear all data

Erases the profile, every task, every thing, and all history on this device. Cannot be undone — export a backup first.
